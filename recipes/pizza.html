Pizza on the Grill

Make your own personal pizza without a pizza oven! Get a rich, smokey taste by cooking it On The Grill!

Topped with tomato sauce, tomatoes, black olives, roasted red peppers, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil, this pizza brings out the most of your grill with savory flavors and fresh ingredients!

Ingredients

Pizza Dough:

  • 1 cup warm watter
  • 1 (0.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 pinch white sugar
  • 3 & 1/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil

Garlic Oil:

  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic

Pizza Toppings:

  • 1/4 cup tomato sauce, divided
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes, divided
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives, divided
  • 1/4 cup roasted red peppers, drained
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh basil, divided

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients
  2. To make the dough: Pour warm water into a large bowl; dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let stand until yeast softens and begins to form a creamy foam, about 5 to 10 minutes.
  3. Mix in flour, 1 tablespoon olive oil, and salt until dough pulls away from the sides of the bowl.
  4. Turn onto a lightly floured surafce. Knead until smooth, about 8 minutes.
  5. Place dough in a well-oiled bowl and cover with a damp cloth.
  6. Set aside to rise until doubled, about 1 hour. Punch down; knead in garlic and basil. Set aside to rise for 1 more hour, or until doubled again.
  7. Meanwhile, make the garlic oil: Combine 1/2 cup olive oil with minced garlic in a microwave-safe cup or bowl. Heat for 30 seconds in the microwave.
  8. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat; brush the grate with garlic oil.
  9. To make the pizzas: PUnch down dough and divide in half. Form each half into an oblong shape 3/8 to 1/2 inch thick.
  10. Carefully place one piece of dough onto the grill. Dough will begin to puff almost immediately. When the bottom crust has lightly browned, turn dough over using two spatulas.
  11. Working quickly, brush garlic oil over crust.
  12. Top with 1/2 of each of the following: tomato sauce, chopped tomatoes, olives, red peppers, cheese, and basil.
  13. Close the lid and cook until cheese melts. REmove from grill and set aside to cool for a few minutes. Repeat with second piece of dough.
  14. Serve hot and enjoy!
